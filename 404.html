Oh Sehun

This is a Korean name; the family name is Oh. Not to be confused with Oh Se-hoon.

Oh Se-hun (born April 12, 1994), better known mononymously as Sehun, is a South Korean dancer, rapper, singer, model and actor. He is a member of the South Korean-Chinese boy group Exo and its sub-group Exo-K. Apart from his group's activities, Sehun has also starred in various television dramas and movies such Busted! (2018), Secret Queen Makers (2018) and Dokgo Rewind (2018).
